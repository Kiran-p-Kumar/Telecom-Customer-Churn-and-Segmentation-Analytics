# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn & Segmentation Analytics

Business-focused Telecom Customer Churn Analytics project using Python, SQL, Machine Learning, and Power BI to identify churn drivers, customer risk segments, revenue loss, and retention opportunities.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges in the telecom industry because losing customers directly impacts recurring revenue, profitability, and customer acquisition costs.

This project analyzes telecom customer behavior to identify:

- High-risk churn customers
- Revenue loss drivers
- Customer retention opportunities
- Contract and service-based churn patterns
- Customer segments contributing to business risk

The project combines:

- Python for analytics
- SQL for business KPI analysis
- Machine Learning for segmentation
- Power BI for dashboard reporting

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Telecom companies experience customer attrition due to:

- Better competitor offers
- Expensive pricing plans
- Poor support experience
- Weak customer engagement
- Service dissatisfaction

The objective of this project is to help businesses:

✔ Identify churn-prone customers  
✔ Understand churn drivers  
✔ Estimate revenue loss  
✔ Segment customers by business value  
✔ Improve customer retention strategies

---

# 📈 Key Findings

| Insight | Observation |
|---|---|
| Overall Churn Rate | 26.54% |
| High-Risk Customers | Month-to-Month customers showed highest churn |
| Revenue Loss | Monthly churn revenue exceeded $137K |
| Internet Service | Fiber Optic customers had higher churn concentration |
| Customer Behavior | Customers with lower tenure churned more frequently |
| Major Churn Drivers | Competitor offers and support dissatisfaction |

---

## 3️⃣ Customer Segmentation

Applied **K-Means Clustering** to segment customers based on:

- Tenure in Months
- Monthly Charges
- Total Charges

### Customer Segments

| Segment | Business Meaning |
|---|---|
| Budget Customers | Lower revenue customers |
| Premium Loyal Customers | High-value long-term customers |
| High-Risk Customers | Customers with high churn probability |
